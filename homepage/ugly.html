About

As a girl with a big heart and bigger dreams, I'm excited to take on the tech sector. When I'm not at my computer, you might find me sketching out design ideas, tending my plants, teaching my cat a new trick, or diving headfirst into a new game. I love a challenge, whether that be a recipe, a new coding language, or taking something apart to figure out how it works.

Experience

I've built all kinds of projects in school on a bunch of platforms, in addition to working multiple positions in restaurants and retail. My most prized projects are a social media Android app for tracking habits and a fast-paced card-throwdown game that won us the "Amazingly Fun to Play and Immediately Want to Play Again" award. Merchandising and plating food has been a great whetstone for my design eye, and my constant exposure to customers has taught me to see things from new perspectives and interpret feedback constructively.
